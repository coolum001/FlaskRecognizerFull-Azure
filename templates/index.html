Australian Urban Small Bird Recognizer

Welcome to the Australian Urban Small Bird Recognizer.

The Australian Museum has a list of the top 30 urban birds, and this website allows you to submit a photograph of a bird, and have the species recognized automatically.

The technical details of the recognizer:

  • Uses a fast.ai Convolution Neural Network;
  • Uses a RESNET50 architecture;
  • Trained on Google Image Search thumbnails of each bird species

Click "Upload" to go to the upload page, click "Home" to return here at any time.
